Frequently Asked Questions

You can reach Pawna lake by private vehicle. There is a parking space for each campsite. Reach Lonavala or Kamshet via train if you are coming by public transport. You need to hire a private jeep from the railway station to the campsite. We will help you with all the transportation.
You will get a separate 2 people, 3 people or 4 people Non-AC tents according to your choice and availability. You don’t have to share a tent with an unknown person. Foam mattresses, bed-sheet, pillow, and blanket will be provided in the tent. (We don’t provide sleeping bags for hygiene purpose)
